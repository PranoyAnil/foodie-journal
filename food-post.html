Creamy Garlic Pasta

Posted on: June 14, 2025

This creamy garlic pasta is rich, satisfying, and takes only 20 minutes to make. Perfect for a quick dinner fix!

Ingredients

    <
  • 200g pasta
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook pasta according to package instructions.
  2. In a pan, melt butter and sauté minced garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add cream, salt, pepper, and stir until heated.
  4. Add pasta to the sauce and toss well.
